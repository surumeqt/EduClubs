Common Questions & Issues

How do I post a commission?

You can post a new commission by logging into your customer dashboard and navigating to the "Post Project" section. Fill in the details, budget, and attach any relevant files.

What if I'm unhappy with the work?

EduClubs offers a dispute resolution process. If you're not satisfied, you can initiate a dispute and our team will help mediate a fair solution.

How do I get paid as a creator?

Once a project is completed and approved by the client, funds are released from escrow to your linked payment method. You can manage this in your creator settings.
